# Animal Friendship
*1st-level, Enchantment*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Target a Beast that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [Charmed](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Charmed) condition for the duration. If you or one of your allies deals damage to the target, the spells ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional Beast for each spell slot level above 1.
