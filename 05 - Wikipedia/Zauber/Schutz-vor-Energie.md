# Protection from Energy
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has resistance to one damage type of your choice: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.

**Classes**: [Cleric](../Charakteroptionen/Klassen/Kleriker.md), [Druid](Dungeons%20&%20Dragons/Wikipedia%20der%20Vergessenen%20Reiche/Kompendium%20der%20Vergessenen%20Reiche/Klassen/druid.md), [Ranger](D&D/05%20-%20Wikipedia/Charakteroptionen/Klassen/Waldläufer.md), [Sorcerer](../Charakteroptionen/Klassen/Zauberer.md), [Wizard](../Charakteroptionen/Klassen/Magier.md)
