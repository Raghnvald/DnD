# Thirsting Blade
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*  

**Prerequisite**: Level 5, Pact of the Blade

You can attack with your pact weapon twice, instead of once, whenever you take the [Attack](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Attack) action on your turn.
