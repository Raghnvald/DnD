# Necklace of Adaptation
*Wondrous Item, major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: Requires Attunement
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While wearing this necklace, you can breathe normally in any environment, and you have advantage on saving throws made against harmful gases and vapors (such as [Todeswolke](../Zauber/Todeswolke.md) and [stinking cloud](../Zauber/Stinkende-Wolke.md) effects, inhaled poisons, and the breath weapons of some dragons).

*Source: SRD / Basic Rules*
