# Wand of Magic Detection
*Wand, minor, uncommon*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This wand has 3 charges. While holding it, you can expend 1 charge as an action to cast the [detect magic](Magie%20entdecken.md) spell from it. The wand regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: SRD / Basic Rules*
