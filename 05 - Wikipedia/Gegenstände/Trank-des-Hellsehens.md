# Potion of Clairvoyance
*Potion, minor, rare*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Hellsehen](../Zauber/Hellsehen.md) spell. An eyeball bobs in this yellowish liquid but vanishes when the potion is opened.
