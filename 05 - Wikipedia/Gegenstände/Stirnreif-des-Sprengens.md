# Circlet of Blasting
*Wondrous Item, major, uncommon*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing this circlet, you can use an action to cast the [scorching ray](Sengender-Strahl.md) spell with it. When you make the spell's attacks, you do so with an attack bonus of +5. The circlet can't be used this way again until the next dawn.

*Source: SRD / Basic Rules*
