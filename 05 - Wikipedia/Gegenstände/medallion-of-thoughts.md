# Medallion of Thoughts
*Wondrous Item, major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: Requires Attunement
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

The medallion has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the [detect thoughts](../Zauber/Gedanken-wahrnehmen.md) spell (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: SRD / Basic Rules*
