# Staff of Healing
*Staff, major, rare (requires attunement by a bard, cleric, or druid)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 B
  - Two-handed: 1d8 B
- **Properties**: Requires Attunement, Versatile
- **Cost**: ‚è§
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

This staff has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spellcasting ability modifier: [cure wounds](../Zauber/Wunden%20heilen.md) (1 charge per spell level, up to 4th), [lesser restoration](../Zauber/Schwache-Genesung.md) (2 charges). or [mass cure wounds](../Zauber/Massen-Wunden-heilen.md) (5 charges).

The staff regains `1d6 + 4` expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a `d20`. On a 1. the staff vanishes in a flash of light, lost forever.
