# Zauberbuch
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 50 Goldmünzen
- **Weight**: 1,5 kg.

Essential for wizards, a spellbook is a leather-bound tome with 100 blank vellum pages suitable for recording spells.

*Source: SRD / Basic Rules*
