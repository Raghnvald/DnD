# Ring of Telekinesis
*Ring, very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-telekinesis.webp#right)


While wearing this ring, you can cast [Telekinesis](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/telekinesis-xphb.md) from it.
