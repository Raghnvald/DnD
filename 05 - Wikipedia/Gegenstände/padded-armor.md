# Padded Armor
*Light armor*  


- **Armor Class**: 11 + Dex modifier
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Cost**: 5 gp
- **Weight**: 8.0 lbs.

Padded armor consists of quilted layers of cloth and batting.
