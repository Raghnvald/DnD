# Potion of Necrotic Resistance
*Potion, uncommon*  


- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you have [Resistance](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to necrotic damage for 1 hour.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 289. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
