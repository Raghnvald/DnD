# Firearm Bullet
*Ammunition*  


- **Cost**: 3 sp
- **Weight**: 0.2 lbs.

The ammunition of a firearm is destroyed upon use.

Firearm Bullets are typically stored in a [Pouch](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/pouch-xphb.md) (bought separately).

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 222*
